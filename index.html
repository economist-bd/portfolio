About the Artist

Md. Monjirul Hoque

Lecturer (Economics) & AI Artist

I am Md. Monjirul Hoque. By profession, I serve as a Lecturer in Economics, but photography is where I find my deepest attraction. Whenever I find a moment of respite from my academic duties, I venture out to capture the world through my lens.

My true joy lies in the fusion of reality and imagination. I love experimenting with my captures using AI technology, transforming simple frames into extraordinary visual experiences. Sharing these experiments on social media and connecting with fellow art enthusiasts is what keeps my creative spirit alive.
